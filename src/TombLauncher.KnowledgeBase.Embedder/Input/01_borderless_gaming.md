# Borderless Gaming

**by billie2001**  
*Posted: 14 Mar 2025 12:46*

---

Borderless gaming is an application that that
gives you the fullscreen experience while playing in windowed mode. It's
like playing in fullscreen without the negative side effects, like the
difficulty of switching to other applications or even crashing the game
if you do.

More info and links to download: <https://github.com/codeusa/borderless-gaming/releases>
or direct download: [https://github.com/Codeusa/Borderless-G ... setup.exe](https://github.com/Codeusa/Borderless-Gaming/releases/download/9.5.6/BorderlessGaming9.5.6_admin_setup.exe)

Version 9.5.6 is free, but there is also a paid version on Steam with additional features, like a migration tool and more.
